Welcome to the Student Portal

Welcome to the Student Portal, your one-stop hub to view and access your grades, lectures, and assessments. To begin, select your department and enter your student ID number to access your academic grades.

For accessing the Student Portfolio, please follow these steps: In the "Select Department" section, choose your department, but do not enter your student ID number. Instead, enter the unique code provided during your class session. After that, choose the type of portfolio you wish to view from the Student Portfolio section to get an in-depth look at your academic materials.

For any concern, Contact the Lecturer
